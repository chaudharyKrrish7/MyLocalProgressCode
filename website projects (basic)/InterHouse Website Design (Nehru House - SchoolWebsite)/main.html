Welcome to St. Michael's Sr. Sec. School

St. Michael's Senior Secondary School is a catholic minority educational institute acknowledged by the National Minorities Commission, New Delhi. Owned, managed and run by the Delhi Catholic Archdiocese, imparting education to students irrespective of their sex, caste, creed and religion to strengthen their inter-communal and inter-cultural ties among the students, so that they learn to respect all religions and cultures of our country and strive to achieve "Unity In Diversity", as part of God's plan for humanity. It is an English medium coeducational school affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education and Directorate of education Haryana. (Affiliation no. 530210 and school code no. 04231) Established in 1954 as a modest endeavor, St. Michael's has been scaling many heights over the years.
